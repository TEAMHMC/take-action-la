About Health Matters Clinic

Community-
Centered

Health Matters Clinic is a Los Angeles-based nonprofit focused on health equity and community-based care. Since 2020, we've served 25,000+ people through mobile health services, pop-up clinics, street medicine outreach, and mental wellness programs — supported by 800+ volunteers.

The Unstoppable Experience is part of the Take Action for Mental Health Awareness Month campaign — a partnership with the LA County Department of Mental Health to bring accessible, stigma-free mental wellness programming directly into our communities.

General About Take Action LA
Take Action LA is an LA County Department of Mental Health (LACDMH) initiative for Mental Health Awareness Month in May 2026. Health Matters Clinic is proud to serve as the community partner bringing three free events to life across the month — all powered by our Unstoppable program. For more information, visit takeactionla.com.
Unstoppable is Health Matters Clinic's flagship year-round mental health and wellness program — workshops, community events, and resources built for real people in real communities.

This May, we're bringing Unstoppable to Take Action LA through three themed events:

MOVE — Live Unstoppable Walk/Run days: community walk/run events that get you moving and connected.
HEAL — Unstoppable Wellness Meetup: an intimate evening of movement, conversation, and self-care.
TRANSFORM — The Unstoppable Experience: a live virtual session built to help you reflect, learn, and grow.

Each event has its own energy and format — all three are free and open to everyone.
There are three events this May. Full details and registration for all three are at healthmatters.clinic/takeactionla.
MOVE — May 9HEAL — May 20TRANSFORM — May 27
Health Matters Clinic (HMC) is a Los Angeles-based nonprofit delivering free healthcare, mental health programming, and wellness resources to underserved communities across LA County. Take Action LA is produced in partnership with the LA County Department of Mental Health.
Yes — all three events are completely free and open to everyone. No cost, no catch. Register at healthmatters.clinic/takeactionla.
Everyone. These events are designed for anyone who lives, works, or cares about community in the LA area. You don't need to be experiencing a crisis or have a mental health diagnosis — Take Action LA is for people who want to move, connect, learn, and invest in their own wellness.
All events are open to participants 18 and older. Minors are welcome when accompanied by a parent or guardian.
MOVE Live Unstoppable Walk/Run · May 9
MOVE is part of our Live Unstoppable Walk/Run series — a free outdoor community event that combines movement with mental health awareness. Whether you walk, jog, or run, you belong here.
Date: Saturday, May 9, 2026
Start Time: 8:00 AM
Location: Curtis Tucker Wellness Center · 123 W Manchester Blvd, Inglewood, CA 90301
Plan to arrive by 8:00 AM — that's when we kick things off with an expert-led warmup and stretch (8:00–8:25 AM) before the walk/run begins. Check-in opens at 7:45 AM if you want to get settled early.
Not at all. This is a community walk/run — all paces are welcome. Walk the whole route, jog, or run. The goal is to show up and move together.
Wear comfortable athletic clothing and supportive shoes. Bring a water bottle. If you registered by May 2nd, watch for your free Unstoppable tee — you may want to wear it.
Free onsite parking is available, and street parking can be found in the surrounding area. Spots fill up fast on event morning — we encourage you to carpool, rideshare, or use Lyft to skip the parking scramble and arrive stress-free.
HEAL Unstoppable Wellness Meetup · May 20
HEAL is the Unstoppable Wellness Meetup — an intimate community evening built around movement, real conversation, self-care stations, and connection. Think live music, a featured wellness conversation, an Ask the Expert session, and hands-on experiences all in one space.
Date: Wednesday, May 20, 2026
Doors open: 5:45 PM
Program: 6:00 PM – 7:15 PM
Location: Curtis Tucker Wellness Center · 123 W Manchester Blvd, Inglewood, CA 90301
The evening includes a movement activation session, a featured Unstoppable conversation, an Ask the Expert segment, and concurrent wellness stations — including a flower station, aromatherapy, massage, and a wellness resource table.
Anyone with questions about health, wellness resources, or navigating care in LA County. This is a live Q&A — a low-barrier way to get real answers in a safe, community setting.
Come as you are. The vibe is welcoming and community-centered — comfortable, casual attire is perfect. There is a brief movement activation, so something you can move in comfortably is a plus.
Free onsite parking is available at the Curtis Tucker Wellness Center, with additional street parking nearby. Rideshare is always a great option — drop-off right at the front and walk in.

Get Involved Volunteer & Ambassadors
There are two ways to plug in:

Unstoppable Ambassador — Spread the word using your personal referral link, tracked through our volunteer portal. The ambassador who gets the most confirmed attendees wins up to a $500 Visa e-gift card.

Core Volunteer — 10 spots available for hands-on event support (3–6 hrs/week for 8 weeks leading up to the events). Apply at volunteer.healthmatters.clinic.
Core Volunteers help execute HMC's outreach and event plans, complete weekly communications tasks, and provide hands-on support on event days. Mandatory attendance at MOVE (May 9) and HEAL (May 20). Skills in event planning, marketing, or community organizing are a plus — training is provided.
Create a free account at volunteer.healthmatters.clinic, then go to Referral Hub to grab your personal ambassador link for each event. Share it on social, in group chats, and in person — every registration through your link is tracked to you.
